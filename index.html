Research

I seek to address next-generation astronomical questions through the development of ambitious data science techniques: those that leverage observations from massive surveys including ZTF and LSST. Research experiences encompassing stellar populations, individual galaxy evolution, galaxy cluster evolution, and telescope operations have led me to draw connections between these fundamental astronomical processes and my future research ambitions. Parellel to research, I am interested in software/pipeline development and open source practices.

Personal Resources

I like to spend my free time reading, dancing, and doing my 'inner work'. By this I mean, becoming aware of my unhealthy feelings or opinions and seeking out the resources with which to grow into greater compassion and kindness. In doing so, I have developed and documented some techniques for how to relate to myself and others in a healthy way, as well as a cultivated a collection of media that has been fundamental to my personal journey.

Always open to book suggestions!
